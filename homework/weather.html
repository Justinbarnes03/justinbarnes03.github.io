Purpose Statement

The purpose of this site is to inform users of current and future weather conditions.

Audience Description

Anyone in the 15-99 year old age range. People who enjoy the outdoors or commute to work. People who make anywhere form $40-100k+ a year. Most users access the website though their mobile. The users are anyone living in the United State. The demographic of the education is very broad, it ranges from high schooler checking the weather for the big game, to the elderly couple checking the weather before they plan their big RV trip.

Audience Persona

  • Greg is 36 years old.
  • He is an avid golfer.
  • "I've always checks the weather before making a tee off time on Saturdays."
  • Greg enjoys outdoor activities with his family.
  • Greg commutes to work everyday to his job, where he sells life insurance.
  • He makes $150,000 a year.
  • He likes to take his wife on a sunday drives in his convertible corvette.
